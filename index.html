About Me

My name is Faith Nswana and I am a Software Engineering student with a strong interest in web development and technology. I enjoy learning how websites and applications are built and how technology can be used to solve real world problems. During my studies I have been developing skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and I am continuing to improve my knowledge in responsive design and modern web development practices. I am also interested in learning more about backend development and full stack technologies in the future. I am always open to learning new tools, improving my skills and collaborating with others on meaningful technology projects.
